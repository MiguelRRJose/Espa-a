Madrid, España

About Madrid

Madrid is the capital of Spain, located in the center of the country. It’s a lively and beautiful city known for its amazing architecture, museums, and delicious food.

Main Attractions

The Royal Palace

One of the most famous landmarks in Madrid.

Retiro Park

A large and peaceful park perfect for walking and relaxing.

Prado Museum

Home to many masterpieces of European art.

Plaza Mayor

A historic square full of life, restaurants, and culture.

Food and Culture

You can enjoy traditional dishes like tapas and churros con chocolate. Madrid’s nightlife is vibrant and full of energy — the city truly never sleeps!

Comparison

Madrid is more relaxed than Paris, but just as beautiful. While Paris is famous for its romance and art, Madrid feels warmer and more energetic. People in Madrid are very friendly, and the city has a lively atmosphere that makes you feel welcome everywhere.
